About The Event

Our team, a committee from the Hispanic Association of Computer Scientists (HACS) and Association of Black Computer Scientists (ABCS), is a focused diverse group of 7 University Of Texas Computer Science students. Our committee is supported by the Computer Science Department, HACS, and ABCS. Our intention is to create a conference, Div Day, to give students in our community the opportunity to have a unique empowering experience so that they can strive for a strengthened diverse community. Our mission is to expose historically marginalized communities interested in tech by providing opportunities to participate in discussions aimed to end the lack of diverse talent. Our aim is to empower our participants to become leaders to enact change in the technology workforce.

HACS

The Hispanic Association of Computer Scientists (HACS) is an organization that aims to empower and provide support for Hispanic students in computer science through academic, professional, and social events.

ABCS

The Association of Black Computer Scientists (ABCS) is a student led organization that provides mentorship, networking, and opportunities to African and African-American students seeking an education in computer science.
